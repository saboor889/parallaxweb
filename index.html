The Xenon-VR Advantage

All-In-One

Various Visual Field functions with Threshold Strategies, Stimulus Sizes, and Goldmann Standard Patters including 10-2,24-2,30-2 and 24-2C

Flexible Testing

Xenon-1 tests are easily customizable based on the patient’s needs and are designed for all ages.

Telehealth Results

Automated evaluation generates results in seconds without compromising the accuracy or quality of patient care.

Optimized Office

Results are instantly recorded in electronic records, exported to a laptop, backed up in the cloud, and are viewable on the mobile device with a SaaS application.

Multilingual

Custom, built-in translation software which allows doctors to communicate effectively with their patients from all corner of the globe

Reimbursement

Doctors can reimburse for each exam they conduct. All exams are billable using existing insurance CPT codes
